
= What are Biomes? =
Biomes are zones in the world of Cubyz that contain specific kinds of blocks and structures. They can range from land biomes such as forests and hills, to underground biomes like caves and rock forests.

Due to Cubyz' lack of a height limit, there can be lots of biomes, all located in different heights. There's even sky biomes (TBD) that have been recently implemented that can be found in certain areas.

Because Cubyz isn't fully released yet, this page can and will grow over time and is meant as a simple directory for all biomes present in-game.
