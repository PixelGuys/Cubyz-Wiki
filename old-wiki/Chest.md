Chests are functional blocks that are able to store items.

== Obtaining ==
Chests can be crafted with 4 planks, producing a chest of the same wood type.

== Usage ==

Interacting with a chest block (<code>placeBlock</code>) opens the Chest UI, where items can be stored. Chests have 20 slots of space.
