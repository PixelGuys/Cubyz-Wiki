Temperate grass is a grass block grown on [[soil]].

== Obtaining ==
Temperate grass is found in many temperate biomes. It is not possible to obtain grass blocks, as when they are broken, they drop their soil block.

== Usage ==
Grass blocks can be broken to obtain their soil block.
