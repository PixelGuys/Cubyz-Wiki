Lanterns are light blocks crafted with torches and iron ingots.

== Obtaining ==
Lanterns can be crafted with 2 torches and 1 iron ingot, producing 2 lanterns matching the type of torch used.

== Usage ==

Lanterns can be placed on the side, top, and bottom of blocks.

Coal lanterns produce a warm yellowish light, while sulfur lanterns produce a blue light. Lanterns are brighter than torches.
