The '''Uranium Ingot''' is a rare and powerful material in Cubyz, prized for its exceptional durability and damage output. Created by smelting uranium ore, this radioactive metal serves as a key component in crafting high-tier tools and equipment.

== Properties ==
Uranium ingots boast impressive statistics that make them highly valuable for adventurers:
* '''High Durability:''' With 1500 durability points, uranium tools last significantly longer than most other materials
* '''Strong Damage Output:''' Deals 10 mass damage and 15 hardness damage, making it effective against tough blocks
* '''Moderate Swing Speed:''' A swing speed of 0.5 provides a balanced trade-off between power and attack rate
