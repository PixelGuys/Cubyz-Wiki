The procedural crafting system consists of a 5x5 matrix of slots. Some slots are inaccessible, some slots are mandatory, and some are optional. The configuration of the slot types depends on the tool preset chosen either [[Pickaxe]], [[Axe]], [[Shovel]], [[Sickle]], or [[Chisel]]. Crafting tools requires materials. Materials must be placed in all mandatory slots and be made into one cohesive object (no floating parts).
