Sky grass is a grass block grown on [[aerosoil]].

== Obtaining ==
Sky grass is only found on sky islands. It is not possible to obtain grass blocks, as when they are broken, they drop their soil block.

== Usage ==
Grass blocks can be broken to obtain their soil block.
