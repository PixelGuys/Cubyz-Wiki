== Obtaining ==
Amber can be obtained from amber ore using pickaxe with damage higher than 15. Amber ore spawns underground below level -1250 and above level -8000. Ore distribution is uniform within that range. Amber ore appears in small veins of around 3 blocks and is one of the most rare ores to encounter so make sure to properly light up caves you are traversing, as  it can appear on ceilings where torch light doesn't reach. When you encounter a vein, make sure to dig ore host blocks and blocks around the ore to ensure you have collected all of it.
